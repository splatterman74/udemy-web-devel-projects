Hello Earth!

A few facts about our spinning blue ball:

Earth is the third planet from the sun and is the largest of the terrestrial planets.
Unlike other planets in the solar system which are named after deities, the earth
is named after the Anglo-Saxon word erda, meaning ground or soil. The earth
was formed approximately 4.5 billion years ago, and is the only known planet
to support life.

About the earth:

  • Mass: 5,972,190,000,000,000 billion kg
  • Diameter at equator: 12,756 km
  • Satellites: The Moon

Top 3 earth facts:

  1. The earth's rotation is gradually slowing.
  2. A year isn't exactly 365 days, and a day isn't exactly 24 hours.
  3. 70% of the earth's surface is water, and only 1/3 of it is fresh water.
